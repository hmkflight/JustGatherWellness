About

Disconnect to reconnect.

Our Mission

Just Gather Wellness exists to help people reclaim their attention, build meaningful connections, and rediscover the power of presence in an increasingly digital world.

What We Believe

Attention is under attack. The constant pull of notifications, feeds, and screens fragments our focus and erodes our capacity for deep connection. We believe presence is a skill that can be cultivated, and that gathering together in intentional ways is essential to human flourishing.

How We Work

Through community gatherings, digital detox retreats, workshops, and youth programs, we create spaces where people can step away from the noise and reconnect with what matters most.
